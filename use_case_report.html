Core Operations

  • Strategic Oversight: Full control over audit trails and system configuration.
  • Inventory lifecycle: Tracking from procurement (Vendors) to storage (Rack/Bin) to sale.
  • Financial Compliance: Automated GST summary generation (CGST/SGST/IGST).

Advanced Features

  • AI Integration: Predictive modeling for demand forecasting and reordering.
  • Automation: Real-time payment verification via Razorpay/UPI webhooks.
  • Gamification: Behavior-driven XP system to incentivize staff productivity.
